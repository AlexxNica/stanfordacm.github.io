The Big Hack is a friendly competitive annual 24-hour hackathon between Berkeley and Stanford. This year it is going to be in the basement of the Huang Engineering building from April 6th to the 7th, 2013.

What: A massive hackathon between Stanford and Cal
When: April 6th-7th from 1PM to 1pm
Where: Basement of Huang Engineering, Stanford

Schedule:

  • Saturday:
    • 12PM - Cal Buses Arrive
    • 1PM - Lunch/Hackathon Begins!
    • 7PM - Dinner
    • 12PM - Midnight BBQ
  • Sunday
    • 8AM - Breakfast is Served
    • 12PM - Lunch is Served
    • 1PM - Hackathon Ends
    • 1.30PM - Demos Begin
    • 4PM - Winners Announced
